[NGT0129] Calibrated soil moisture data from Manaus, Brazil, August 2018 - December 2019

Author(s): Negron-Juarez R; Monteiro T; Araujo A; Candido L; Faybishenko B; Gimenez B; Chambers J; Newman B; Warren J; Ferreira S; Oliveira R; Varadharajan C; Agarwal D; Borma L


Dataset Information

Site ID: BR-Ma2

Site Name: Manaus - ZF2 K34

Variables: Soil water content (saturation)

Date Range: Sept. 1, 2018 - Dec. 31, 2019

Description: This dataset contains calibrated soil moisture and rainfall data for ecohydrological studies in the Central Amazon (2.6099 ºS, 60.2093 ºW) taken at 30-min intervals. Soil moisture data was collected at depths of 0.025-, 0.05-, 0.15-, 0.30-, 0.50-,0.8-, 1.6-, 2.4-, 3.2-, 4.8-, 6.4-, 8.8-, and 14.20 m. Inconsistent data was removed during the QA/QC process. Further information including data collection methods are available in the associated publication, Negron-Juarez et al. 2020. Measurement and characterization of soil moisture dynamics in a Central Amazonian tropical forest (to be submitted).

QA/QC: Full QA-QC

Methods Description: Please see the associated publication for a description of methods used: Negron-Juarez et al. 2020. Measurement and characterization of soil moisture dynamics in a Central Amazonian tropical forest (to be submitted)

Access Level: NGEE Tropics
